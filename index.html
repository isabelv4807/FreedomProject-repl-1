Forensic science is science that is applied to solving illegal or criminal cases. Forensic scientists collect, gather, and observe different types of evidence located at the crime scene or on the suspects and victims. They must acquire skills in chemistry, biology, and physics to help them analyze and critically think on the job. There are several different branches of forensic science each with their own set of skills required. Scientist in one field will not be able to do the job of another because each branch of forensic science requires different procedures and analytical skills.
